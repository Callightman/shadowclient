Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shadow (“the app”, “we”) handles information. Shadow is a “bring your own server” proxy and VPN client for iOS and Apple TV. It does not sell, provide, or operate proxy/VPN servers, and it does not require an account.

The short version

  • We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not see, store, or log your browsing activity or network traffic.
  • There is no account, no login, and no in-app purchases.
  • Your configurations and settings stay on your device.

Information stored on your device

The app stores the following locally on your device, or in the app’s private App Group container so its system extensions (the VPN tunnel, widget, and share extension) can access it:

  • Proxy/VPN configurations you import (subscription URLs, QR codes, config files, or manually entered nodes), including their server addresses and credentials.
  • Routing rules, app preferences (such as language and appearance), and the currently selected node.
  • Connection diagnostics and node health/test results, used to help you troubleshoot.

This information is never transmitted to us. We have no servers that receive it.

Network traffic

To route your traffic, the app uses Apple’s Network Extension (Packet Tunnel) framework. When you connect, your traffic is tunneled to the proxy or VPN server that you supplied. We do not operate those servers, and we do not have access to, monitor, or record the contents of your traffic. The privacy and logging practices of the server you choose are governed by whoever operates it — not by us.

Subscriptions you import

If you import a subscription URL, the app contacts that URL directly from your device to download the node list you requested. This request goes to the provider you chose, not to us.

Data we collect

None. The app contains no analytics, no crash-reporting SDK, no advertising identifier, and no tracking. We do not build user profiles and we do not perform any tracking as defined by Apple’s App Tracking Transparency.

Children

Shadow is a general-purpose networking utility and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
