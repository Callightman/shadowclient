What Shadow does

Import in seconds

Subscription link, QR code, config file, or paste. Reads sing-box, Xray, v2ray, Clash, and WireGuard formats.

More protocols

VLESS, VMess, Trojan, Shadowsocks, Hysteria2, TUIC, WireGuard, SSH, AnyTLS, ShadowTLS — with Reality, ECH, and TCP Brutal.

Plain-language diagnostics

Tells you whether a problem is the app, your config, your server, or your network — not just raw logs.

Built to feel simple

Guided onboarding, debuggable routing rules, a home-screen widget, dark mode, Apple TV, and English / Turkish / Chinese.
